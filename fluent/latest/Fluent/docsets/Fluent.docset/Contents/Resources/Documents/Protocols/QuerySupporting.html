Aggregate
  • QueryAggregate

    Aggregates generate data for every row of returned data. They usually aggregate data for a single field, but can also operate over most fields. When an aggregate is applied to a query, the aggregate method will apply to all rows filtered by the query, but only one row (the aggregate) will actually be returned.

    The most common use of aggregates is to get the count of columns.

    let count = User.query(on: ...).count()

    They can also be used to generate sums or averages for all values in a column.
